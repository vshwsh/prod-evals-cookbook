# Product Roadmap 2025

**Last Updated:** January 10, 2025  
**Owner:** Product Team

## Vision

In 2025, Acme Projects becomes the **intelligent project management platform** - not just a place to track tasks, but an AI-powered assistant that helps teams work smarter.

## Annual Themes

### Q1: Foundation for AI
- Launch AI infrastructure and data pipelines
- Release AI-powered search across all content
- Beta: Smart task suggestions

### Q2: Automation Everywhere
- Workflow automation builder (no-code)
- AI-generated status updates
- Smart notifications that reduce noise

### Q3: Enterprise Scale
- Advanced permissions and audit logs
- SSO group sync (auto-provisioning)
- Custom fields and workflows for enterprise

### Q4: Intelligence Layer
- Predictive project health scores
- AI meeting assistant integration
- Cross-project insights and recommendations

## Dependencies & Risks

### Technical Dependencies
- AI infrastructure must be complete before Q1 features
- Mobile performance fix needed before offline improvements
- SSO group sync requires identity provider partnerships

### Risks
- **AI quality:** If suggestions aren't good, adoption will suffer
- **Enterprise timeline:** SSO group sync has complex edge cases
- **Mobile team capacity:** Small team with large backlog
