# Remote Work Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** People Operations

## Overview

Acme Corp is a remote-first company. All employees have the option to work fully remotely, from the office, or a hybrid arrangement based on their preferences and role requirements.

## Core Hours

To facilitate collaboration across time zones, all employees should be available during **core hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM Pacific Time**, Monday through Friday. Outside of core hours, employees have flexibility to structure their work day as needed.

## Home Office Setup

### Equipment Provided
- Laptop (refreshed every 3 years)
- Monitor (one 27" display)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset for video calls

### Home Office Stipend
All remote employees receive a **$500 annual stipend** for home office expenses. This can be used for:
- Ergonomic furniture (desk, chair)
- Internet upgrade
- Office supplies
- Lighting or other workspace improvements

The stipend is paid in January each year and does not roll over.

## Communication Expectations

### Response Times
- **Slack messages:** Respond within 4 hours during core hours
- **Email:** Respond within 24 hours
- **Urgent issues:** Use phone or Slack with @mention

### Video Meetings
- Camera on is encouraged but not required
- Use virtual backgrounds if preferred
- Mute when not speaking

## In-Office Days

For employees near our San Francisco office, we encourage (but don't require) in-office presence on **Tuesdays and Thursdays** for team collaboration. The office is available Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 6 PM.

## International Remote Work

Employees may work from international locations for up to **4 weeks per calendar year** with manager approval. Longer arrangements require HR and Legal review for tax implications.
