# Customer Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** October 1, 2024  
**Policy Owner:** Finance & Customer Success

## Overview

Acme Corp is committed to customer satisfaction. This policy outlines when and how customers may receive refunds for Acme Projects subscriptions.

## Eligibility

### Full Refunds
Customers are eligible for a **full refund** if:
- They cancel within **30 days** of initial subscription
- They experience a service outage exceeding our SLA commitments (99.9% uptime)
- They were incorrectly billed due to our error

### Pro-Rata Refunds
Customers are eligible for a **pro-rata refund** if:
- They cancel an annual subscription mid-term (after the 30-day window)
- They downgrade from a higher tier mid-billing cycle

### No Refunds
Refunds are **not available** for:
- Monthly subscriptions (cancel anytime, no refund for partial months)
- Usage-based charges already consumed
- Add-on purchases (storage, API calls) already used

## Refund Process

### For Customers
1. Contact support@acme-corp.com or use the in-app chat
2. Provide your account email and reason for refund request
3. Our team will respond within **2 business days**
4. Approved refunds are processed within **5-7 business days**

### For Customer Success Team
1. Verify eligibility in the customer's account history
2. Calculate refund amount (use the Refund Calculator in Admin)
3. Submit refund request in Stripe with reason code
4. Log the refund in Salesforce opportunity
5. Notify the customer via email

## Refund Approval Matrix

| Refund Amount | Approver |
|---------------|----------|
| Under $500 | CS Representative |
| $500 - $5,000 | CS Manager |
| $5,000 - $25,000 | VP Customer Success |
| Over $25,000 | CFO |

## Exceptions

Enterprise customers with custom contracts should refer to their specific agreement terms. Master Service Agreements (MSAs) may have different refund terms.

## Metrics & Reporting

The Finance team tracks refund metrics monthly:
- Total refund amount
- Refund rate (% of revenue)
- Refunds by reason category
- Average time to process

Target: Keep refund rate under **2% of monthly revenue**.
